## 1. What we collect

- **Account information** — your name, email, password (hashed), and language and
  timezone preferences.
- **Contact details** — any phone, address, or website you choose to add.
- **Business & inventory data** — businesses you create or join, locations, bins,
  stock levels, and movements.
- **Activity data** — sign-in history (including IP address and device), emails we
  send you, and any feedback or support requests you submit.

## 2. How we use it

To operate and secure the Service, provide support, communicate with you, and
improve the product.

## 3. Legal basis

We process your data to provide the Service you requested (performance of a
contract), to meet legal obligations, and for our legitimate interests in running
and securing the Service.

## 4. Subprocessors

We rely on a small number of service providers to operate Balloonventory:

- **Resend** — outbound email delivery.
- **Our hosting provider** — application and database hosting.
- **Stripe** — payment processing (only once paid plans launch).

## 5. Data sources

Our product catalog is aggregated from manufacturer charts, distributor listings
(such as BargainBalloons, LA Balloons, and Larocks), and legacy data. It is
provided for identification, not as an authoritative source of pricing or
availability.

## 6. Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your
account it is deactivated immediately; residual personal data is purged on a
defined schedule. A business with no remaining owner is frozen and may be purged
after a defined period.

## 7. Your rights

You can access and update most of your information in the app. To request a copy
of your data, or its deletion, contact us at the address below and we will respond
within a reasonable period (within 30 days where required by law).

Note: a business's inventory data belongs to the business, not to an individual
member. A personal data request covers your personal data; business-data requests
are handled with the business owner.

## 8. Cookies

We use only strictly necessary cookies plus some local storage for preferences.
See the [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details.
