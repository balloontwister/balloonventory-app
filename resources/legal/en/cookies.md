
## Cookies we use

Balloonventory uses only **strictly necessary** cookies — the ones required for
the app to function and stay secure:

- **Session cookie** — keeps you signed in.
- **CSRF / XSRF token** — protects forms against cross-site request forgery.
- **Locale** — remembers your language choice.

## Local storage

We also use your browser's local storage for interface preferences (for example,
which view or columns you last used, and whether you've dismissed a one-time
notice). Local storage is not a cookie and stays on your device.

## No tracking or marketing cookies

We do **not** use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies. Because
we only set strictly necessary cookies, no consent banner gate is required — we
show a one-time informational notice instead.

## Changes

If we ever add analytics or marketing cookies, we will update this policy and add
a proper consent mechanism first.
